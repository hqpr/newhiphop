Download the new project from Tha Joker Too Cold "Meanwhile."

The Joker comes through today with a new mixtape, Meanwhile, which he's been leading up to for some time. We've premiered a few cuts off the tape, including "Run It" and "Slight Work." The mixtape serves as a warm-up to his forthcoming EP Robert Frost, but nonetheless it's solid with all original production coming from Big Fruit, Tekneek Beats, DoughboyBeatz and more.

Features come from Dizzy Wright, Don Trip, Lil Mal, Big Fruit and others. 

Listen/download the mixtape and let us know what you think. Will it make it into your iTunes rotation?
